Unisphere aims to gather various information related to universities all around the world. Users will easily access all universities around the world and they will be able to compare the universities side by side on a user-friendly interface. Besides the educational aspects, by using Google Street view, users can travel inside the campus and they will be able to see the close by facilities such as hospitals, shopping malls, airport, subway. They can learn how to go nearby places by the color-coded fastest routes displayed on an interactive map.
